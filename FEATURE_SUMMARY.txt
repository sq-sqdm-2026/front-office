BROADCAST RIGHTS & STADIUM UPGRADE FEATURES - SUMMARY
================================================================================

WHAT WAS BUILT
--------------
Two major Baseball Mogul-style owner management features for the Front Office
baseball simulator:

1. TV BROADCAST RIGHTS NEGOTIATION
   - Negotiate broadcast deals with different revenue/restriction tradeoffs
   - 4 deal types: Standard, Premium Cable, Streaming, Blackout
   - Market-based value calculation ($35M-$100M/year range)
   - Annual deal year decrements with auto-reset when expired
   - Fan loyalty impacts (-5 to 0 depending on deal type)

2. STADIUM UPGRADES
   - Capital investment system for stadium improvements
   - 5 upgrades: Luxury Suites, Jumbotron, Concourse, Field, Retractable Roof
   - Costs: $5M-$50M
   - Recurring annual revenue: $500K-$5M/year
   - Fan loyalty bonuses: +2 to +10

================================================================================
BROADCAST RIGHTS SYSTEM DETAILS
================================================================================

DEAL TYPES AND CHARACTERISTICS
------------------------------
Standard Regional:
  - Multiplier: 1.0x
  - Duration: 3 years
  - Value: $35-55M/year
  - Loyalty: No impact
  - Best for: Conservative teams

Premium Cable:
  - Multiplier: 1.3x
  - Duration: 5 years (locked)
  - Value: $50-80M/year
  - Loyalty: No impact
  - Best for: Teams wanting stability and good revenue

Streaming Exclusive:
  - Multiplier: 1.2x
  - Duration: 4 years
  - Value: $40-70M/year
  - Loyalty: -5 per deal
  - Best for: Teams willing to trade some fan loyalty for revenue

Blackout Package:
  - Multiplier: 1.5x
  - Duration: 3 years
  - Value: $60-100M/year
  - Loyalty: -10 per deal, -10 per season
  - Best for: Desperate teams needing maximum revenue

VALUE CALCULATION
-----------------
base_value = (market_size * 8,000,000) + (fan_base * 200,000)
deal_value = base_value * deal_multiplier
result = clamp(deal_value, deal_type_min, deal_type_max)

Example: Market 3 team with 50 fan base:
  base = (3 * 8M) + (50 * 200K) = 24M + 10M = 34M
  premium_cable = 34M * 1.3 = 44.2M (then clamped to 50-80M range = 50M)

SEASON ADVANCEMENT
------------------
At end of each season (offseason day 91):
1. Decrement broadcast_deal_years_remaining by 1
2. If years == 0, reset to standard deal with 3 years
3. If deal is blackout, apply -10 fan loyalty penalty

================================================================================
STADIUM UPGRADE SYSTEM DETAILS
================================================================================

UPGRADE TYPES AND CHARACTERISTICS
--------------------------------
Luxury Suites:
  - Cost: $15M
  - Annual Revenue: $3M
  - ROI: 5 years
  - Fan Loyalty: +2

Jumbotron/Tech:
  - Cost: $8M
  - Annual Revenue: $1.5M
  - ROI: 5.3 years
  - Fan Loyalty: +5

Concourse Renovation:
  - Cost: $12M
  - Annual Revenue: $2M
  - ROI: 6 years
  - Fan Loyalty: +3

Field Renovation:
  - Cost: $5M
  - Annual Revenue: $500K
  - ROI: 10 years
  - Fan Loyalty: +2

Retractable Roof:
  - Cost: $50M
  - Annual Revenue: $5M
  - ROI: 10 years
  - Fan Loyalty: +10
  - Best investment overall

PURCHASE MECHANICS
------------------
1. Team must have sufficient cash
2. Each upgrade can only be purchased once per team
3. Cost deducted from team cash immediately
4. Fan loyalty impact applied immediately
5. Annual revenue starts next season
6. All upgrades tracked in stadium_upgrades_json

REVENUE INTEGRATION
-------------------
Stadium upgrade revenue is added to local revenue:
local_revenue = ticket + concessions + merchandise + stadium_upgrades
total_revenue = local_revenue + broadcast_revenue

This makes stadium improvements have immediate financial impact.

================================================================================
FINANCIAL INTEGRATION
================================================================================

BROADCAST REVENUE CALCULATION
-----------------------------
Before:
  broadcast_revenue = market_base * broadcast_contract_multiplier

After:
  IF broadcast_deal_value > 0:
    broadcast_revenue = broadcast_deal_value
  ELSE:
    broadcast_revenue = market_base * contract_multiplier (legacy)

STADIUM REVENUE CALCULATION
---------------------------
Added to local revenue calculation:
  stadium_upgrade_revenue = SUM(annual_revenue for each purchased upgrade)
  local_revenue += stadium_upgrade_revenue

SEASON FINANCES
---------------
Called at end of season (offseason day 91):
1. Calculate all revenue including broadcast and stadium
2. Calculate total expenses
3. Calculate profit (revenue - expenses)
4. Update team cash
5. Update franchise valuation
6. Process broadcast deal expiration/penalty

================================================================================
FRONTEND INTEGRATION
================================================================================

FINANCES SCREEN CARDS
---------------------
1. Broadcast Rights Card
   - Shows: Current deal, annual value, years remaining
   - List of all available deals with estimated values
   - Negotiate buttons for each deal type
   - Disabled if current deal hasn't expired

2. Stadium Management Card
   - Shows: Stadium name, year, capacity, condition
   - Annual revenue from upgrades (if any)
   - List of all 5 upgrades
   - Buy buttons for unpurchased upgrades
   - Owned label for purchased upgrades

API CALLS
---------
GET /finances/{team_id}/broadcast-status
  Returns current deal + all available options

POST /finances/{team_id}/broadcast-deal
  body: {"deal_type": "standard|premium_cable|streaming|blackout"}

GET /finances/{team_id}/stadium
  Returns stadium status + all available upgrades

POST /finances/{team_id}/stadium-upgrade
  body: {"upgrade_key": "luxury_suites|jumbotron|concourse|field_renovation|retractable_roof"}

USER INTERACTION
----------------
1. Team manager views Finances screen
2. In Broadcast Rights card, clicks Negotiate button
3. Deal is negotiated, revenue increases, fan loyalty may decrease
4. In Stadium Management card, clicks Buy button
5. Upgrade is purchased (if team has cash), revenue boost applied
6. At season end, broadcast deals decrement and reset if expired
